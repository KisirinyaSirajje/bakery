About Us

Learn more about Sweet Things

Our Story

Sweet Things was founded on March 15th 2025 by a group of passionate bakers who saw the need for quality, artisanal bread in the Makerere community. What started as a small operation with just a few bread varieties has grown into a beloved bakery offering a wide range of breads, pastries, cakes, and cookies.

Our bakery is located in the heart of Makerere, just a short walk from the university campus. We take pride in using traditional baking methods combined with innovative recipes to create products that delight our customers.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that we use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients in everything we bake. We believe that good bread starts with good ingredients, and we're committed to supporting our local community.

Our Mission

At Sweet Things, our mission is to bring joy to our community through delicious, freshly baked goods made with love and care. We strive to:

  • Create exceptional baked goods using traditional methods and the finest ingredients
  • Provide a warm, welcoming environment for our customers
  • Support local farmers and suppliers
  • Contribute positively to the Makerere community
  • Continuously innovate while respecting baking traditions
