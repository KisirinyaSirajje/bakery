Welcome to Sweet Things

At Sweet Things, we believe that good bread is the foundation of a good meal. Located in the vibrant community of Makerere, our bakery combines traditional baking methods with innovative recipes to create bread and pastries that delight the senses.

Every day, our skilled bakers wake up at dawn to prepare fresh, aromatic bread that has become a staple in many Ugandan homes. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure that every bite is as nutritious as it is delicious.
